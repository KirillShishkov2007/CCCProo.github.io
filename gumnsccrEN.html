VIDEO ANTHEM OF THE USSR 1972-1992

TEXT OF THE ANTHEM OF THE USSR 1972-1992

Indestructible Union of the free republics

Great Russia united forever.

Long live created by the will of the peoples

United, mighty Soviet Union!

Hail, our free Fatherland,

Friendship of peoples is a reliable stronghold!

Lenin's party is the power of the people

Leads us to the triumph of communism!

The sun of freedom shone through the thunderstorms,

And the great Lenin illuminated our path:

He raised the nations to a just cause,

He inspired us to work and deeds!

Hail, our free Fatherland,

Friendship of peoples is a reliable stronghold!

Lenin's party is the power of the people

Leads us to the triumph of communism!

In the victory of the immortal ideas of communism

We see the future of our country,

And the Red Banner of the Glorious Motherland

We will always be selflessly loyal!

Hail, our free Fatherland,

Friendship of peoples is a reliable stronghold!

Lenin's party is the power of the people

Leads us to the triumph of communism!
